FAQs and Safety

Are helmets provided?

Yes, we prioritize your safety. Helmets are provided with every scooter rental free of charge.

Do I need a driver's license?

Yes, a valid driver's license is required to rent our scooters.

What if I have a mechanical issue?

Our service team is available to assist you with any mechanical issues. Simply contact us, and we'll provide support.

Can I ride the scooter on the beach?

For safety reasons, we recommend avoiding riding scooters on the beach, as sand and uneven terrain can pose risks.

Wear Protective Gear

Always wear a helmet and appropriate protective gear, such as closed-toe shoes and long pants, to minimize injury risk.

Follow Traffic Rules

Obey all traffic laws and signals. Remember, scooters are considered vehicles and must follow the same rules as cars.

Inspect Your Scooter

Before riding, check the scooter for any visible damage or mechanical issues. Report any concerns to our service team.

Be Aware of Surroundings

Stay alert and aware of your surroundings while riding. Watch out for pedestrians, other vehicles, and obstacles.
